How to Make High-End Grilled Cheese

Level up your grilled cheese game with just a couple tips!

Most people know how to simply make a grilled cheese, but few know how to MAKE a grilled cheese.

Follow the recipe below to make the best grilled cheese you have ever had!

Ingredients:

  • White Bread
  • Butter
  • Havarti
  • Swiss
  • Pepper Jack

Steps:

  1. Heat up frying pan on medium heat, add butter to pan
  2. Add one slice of bread, top with cheeses
  3. Add top piece of bread, cook 3 min on first side
  4. Flip and cook on the other side 2 min until browned on both sides
  5. Enjoy!
