How to Cook Wood Fired Pizza with Ooni

Make a Punch Pizza quality pie with an Ooni pizza oven

You might think you need to fly to Napoli to have a half decent pizza, but that isn't true. With an Ooni oven, you can make a legit wood fired pizza from the comfort of your home.

Follow the steps below to make a restaurant quality neopolitan pizza.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil
  • Marinara

Steps:

  1. Roll out the pizza dough, form into a small circle about a half inch thick
  2. Spread marinara sauce over the base
  3. Split up Mozzarella into small chunks and spread over pizza
  4. Heat up pizza oven to 400 degrees
  5. Add pizza and cook for 10 minutes
