How to Grill an Awesome Brat

Grilling brats is easy for any skill level!

Are you looking to level up your grilling game? Are you tired of making sad boiled hotdogs day in and day out? NO MORE!

This guide will show you just how easy it is to grill up an awesome brat that tastes like Bobby Flay grilled it himself. Read on to learn how!

Ingredients

  • Brats
  • Buns
  • Mustard
  1. Fire up your grill, let it preheat at high temp for 10 minutes. After grates are hot, scrape the grates to clean them
  2. Turn burners down to low, add brats to the grill and space them apart
  3. Every couple minutes, turn each brat slightly. Continue to cook for around 20 minutes while continuing to rotate every 2 or 3 minutes until browned all over
  4. Add your buns to the grill for 1 minute to toast
  5. Add brats to buns, top with mustard
